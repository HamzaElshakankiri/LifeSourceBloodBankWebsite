    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to frequently asked questions.

    • How do I donate?

      Log-in to book an available appointments. If you do not have an account, please create an account, by signing-up.

    • How do I know if I am eligible to donate?

      During the process of booking an appointment, donors will fill out a questionare. This questionare will determine if you are eligible to donate. We ask users to provide accurate information. Once you arrive at the donation appointment, you will be asked to fill out offical medical forms.

    • I don't know my blood type; can I still donate?

      Yes, simply select that you do not know your blood type, during the sign-up process. Once you complete your first donation, you will be informed of what type your blood is. Then, you can add your blood type by editting your profile.

    • Is there a minimum age for donation?

      Yes, the minimum age for donation in Canada is 17.

    • What is the minimum time between blood donations?

      56 days for males.
      84 days for females.

    • What is the best way to contact Life Source Blood Bank?

      The best way to contact Life Source Blood Bank is through our email (LifeSourceBloodBankadm@gmail.com). Other contact information listed on our website, such as address and phone number, are for the University of Regina, and not for our website directly.
